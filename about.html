Dr. Brandon Prusa

Dr. Prusa completed his graduate studies at the prestigious University of Missouri-Kansas City Dental School. While in dental school, he quickly advanced as a strong clinician in the cosmetic realm of dentistry. He was the winner of the prestigious Excellence in Cosmetic Dentistry’ award given out to a select few during their studies.

In his spare time, Dr. Prusa enjoys traveling, running, tennis, and reading. Dr. Prusa is also a huge Blackhawks fan.

Dr. John Vinckier

Dr. Vinckier graduated from the University of Michigan School of Dentistry in Ann Arbor, Michigan. Dr. Vinckier previously practiced in a community dental clinic setting that provided dental services for patient’s of all ages.

Dr. John Vinckier enjoys spending his free time with family and friends. He also loves exploring the beautiful city of Chicago and all it has to offer. He is the middle of five children and both his mother and eldest sister are both practicing dentists in his home state of Michigan.

Dr. Cody Wilfinger

Cody Wilfinger, DDS is a native of Michigan and grew up in the Metro Detroit area. He received his undergraduate degree and Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from the University of Detroit Mercy. Upon graduation from dental school, he received an award for outstanding achievement in operative dentistry from the Academy of Operative Dentistry.

In his spare time, Dr. Wilfinger enjoys exploring Chicago’s food scene, traveling, spending time with friends and family, and enjoying the outdoors with his dog Harper! Dr. Wilfinger is a member of the American Dental Association, Illinois State Dental Society, and the Chicago Dental Society.
